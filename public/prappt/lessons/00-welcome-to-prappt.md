## What is PrAPPt?

The **PrAPPt Framework** is a structured approach to building [[AI Partners]] through [[Response Engineering]]. Unlike treating AI as a tool, PrAPPt helps you create consistent, reliable collaborators that understand their role and deliver predictable results.

The name comes from its six core components:

- **P**ERSONA - Who the AI partner is
- **R**EGULATION - How they must behave
- **A**PPROACH - Their methodology
- **P**URPOSE - What they're designed to do
- **P**RODUCT - What they deliver
- **t**rigger - How you activate them

## Why PrAPPt Matters

Traditional prompting is like giving one-off instructions. PrAPPt is like writing an operating system for an [[AiBou]] (AI partner). Once defined, your AiBou:

- Maintains consistency across conversations
- Understands context and boundaries
- Produces predictable, high-quality outputs
- Can be enhanced and versioned over time

## Core Components Explained

### PERSONA
Defines **who** your AiBou is. Not just a role, but their expertise, stance, and understanding of your partnership.

*Example:* "You are KCivWebWeaver, a civil engineer for digital infrastructure who makes publishing invisible."

### PURPOSE
States **what** your AiBou is designed to accomplish. Clear success criteria and deliverables.

*Example:* "Build KYOUDAI.dev into a fully functional website with three autonomous zones."

### APPROACH
Details **how** your AiBou works. Their methodology, phases, and decision-making process.

### REGULATION
Establishes **boundaries**. What they must do, must not do, and how they handle edge cases.

*Example:* "Never defer with TODOs. Build complete, production-ready code."

### PRODUCT
Specifies **outputs**. Exactly what artifacts, formats, and deliverables to produce.

*Example:* "Complete HTML, CSS, JS for all three zones. Fully responsive. Drop-in ready."

### trigger
The **activation word** or phrase that embodies your AiBou in conversation.

*Example:* `[embody] KCivWebWeaver`
